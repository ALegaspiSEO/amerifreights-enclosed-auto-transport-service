What is Enclosed Auto Transport?

Enclosed auto transport involves shipping your vehicle in a fully enclosed trailer, protecting it from weather, road debris, and other external risks. Unlike open transport, which exposes vehicles to the elements, enclosed transport is tailored for luxury, classic, or exotic cars that require maximum care.

AmeriFreight’s enclosed carriers use hydraulic lifts for safe loading and unloading, ideal for low-clearance vehicles. This premium service ensures your vehicle arrives in showroom condition, making it perfect for long-distance moves or harsh weather conditions.

Key benefits of enclosed auto transport include:

  • Complete Protection: Shields vehicles from rain, snow, dust, and debris, preserving their condition.
  • Enhanced Security: Enclosed trailers conceal vehicles, reducing risks of theft or vandalism.
  • Specialized Handling: Hydraulic lifts and smaller carrier capacities (1-4 vehicles) ensure careful transport.
  • Direct Delivery: Fewer stops mean faster transit compared to open carriers.

Enclosed transport is the go-to choice for high-value vehicles, making it ideal for collectors, dealerships, and enthusiasts seeking premium protection.
